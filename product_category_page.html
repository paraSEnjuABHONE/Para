Products Category

Clothing

The Myanmar longyi, a traditional sarong-like garment, has been a part of Burmese attire for centuries, characterized by its versatility, comfort, and vibrant patterns. Worn by both men and women, it reflects the cultural diversity and timeless style of Myanmar.

Cosmetic

Myanmar's rich jewelry tradition dates back to ancient times, with exquisite pieces featuring prized gemstones like jade, rubies, and sapphires. These gems have cultural and spiritual significance, making Myanmar a renowned hub for high-quality jewelry craftsmanship.

Cosmetic

Myanmar's cosmetic heritage is deeply rooted in thanaka, a natural paste made from the bark of the thanaka tree. This cosmetic practice, which spans generations, not only serves as a beauty enhancer but also symbolizes cultural identity and sun protection in Myanmar..
