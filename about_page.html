History of Longyi

The Myanmar longyi, a traditional garment, has a rich history dating back centuries in myanmar. Myanmar Longyi serves as a versatile and culturally significant piece of clothing, reflecting Myanmar's diverse regional styles and weaving techniques, and is worn by people of all ages for various occasions.They are not just clothing items but also symbols of Myanmar.

History of Ruby

Myanmar's jewelry-making tradition is deeply rooted in its history, featuring exquisite pieces adorned with precious gemstones like jade, rubies, and sapphires. These jewelry items have been cherished for generations, representing both ornamental beauty and spiritual significance, often handed down as heirlooms connect families to cultural heritage.

History of Thanaka

Thanaka, a natural cosmetic derived from the bark of the thanaka tree, is an iconic element of Burmese beauty rituals. Used for thousands of years, thanaka serves both cosmetic and cultural purposes, adorning the faces of Burmese women with intricate designs during daily life and religious ceremonies, symbolizing Myanmar's cultural heritage and natural practices.
